Overview

The Allison family story begins in late‑18th‑century Scotland and unfolds across the Atlantic in Cape Breton, Nova Scotia. Over generations, the family transitioned from Scottish Lowland roots to maritime livelihoods, and ultimately to coal mining communities that defined industrial Cape Breton.

This history traces the direct Allison line using church records, census data, and documented family research, prioritizing evidence over oral tradition.

Scottish Origins

William Allison Sr. & Jean Stewart

William Allison Sr., born around 1785, lived in the Inverkip and Greenock region of Scotland. He married Jean Stewart on 10 August 1811 in Greenock.

Jean Stewart was the daughter of James Stewart and Isobel Niven, placing the family firmly within documented Lowland Scottish parish records.

A Transatlantic Generation

William Allison Jr.

Born on 7 August 1812 in Inverkip, Scotland, William Allison Jr. became a sailor and fisherman. His work connected Scotland with Atlantic Canada through seasonal fishing routes.

Louise Fougeau

During this period, William Allison Jr. formed a relationship with Louise Fougeau, an Acadian French woman from the Cape Breton region. This union introduced Acadian ancestry into the Allison line.

Establishment in Cape Breton

Francis Allison

Francis Allison was born in 1858 in Cape Breton, Nova Scotia, confirming that the Allison family had permanently crossed the Atlantic by the mid‑19th century.

Marriage to Mary Ann Campbell

Francis married Mary Ann Campbell on 21 February 1886 at St. Joseph’s Catholic Church in North Sydney. Her parents were Alexander Campbell and Rachel Young.

The Coal Mining Era

John James Allison

Born on 21 April 1885 in Sydney Mines, John James Allison entered the coal mining industry that defined Cape Breton’s economy. He later settled in Glace Bay.

Marriage to Maria York

John James married Maria York, whose English ancestry added another cultural layer to the family line.

The Modern Connection

Rita Artis Allison

Born around 1918 in Glace Bay, Rita Artis Allison married Howard Frederick Burchell on 9 May 1949, carrying the Allison line into the present generation.
